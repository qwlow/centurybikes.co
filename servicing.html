FAQ

Will suspensions and dropper posts be serviced?

We do not service suspensions or dropper posts.

Can you fix sound issues in servicing?

Most creaking sounds come from old or damaged bearings which can be fixed in a full service. In more severe cases where the source cannot be identified, an overhaul may be neccessary.

Do you sell replacement parts?

We stock up on parts that wear out quickly, such as cassette, chain, bar tape, grips, tubes etc. If we don't have what you're looking for, feel free to buy them elsewhere and bring it along with your bike for us to install.
