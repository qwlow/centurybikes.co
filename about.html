Our Mission

An ex-Shimano Engineer with years of experience servicing road and mountain bikes, I started Century Bikes in 2019 to provide affordable alternatives to retail bicycle shops while still upholding the same quality of work. In only a few short years, we've been fortunate to have built a strong reputation that we continuously strive to uphold everyday. While running a home based business comes with many challenges, seeing our customers satisfied makes it all worth it. Join us on our journey today!
